More About Me

Going back to my interest about cars, I've been a fan of motorsport from a young age. I had a little Porsche Carrera GT toy, so I started following Porsche's racing teams around the world, and then I found out they used to make engines for Formula One teams. I took an interest for Formula One a couple years ago and now I watch the races during the weekends. My favorite team is Scuderia Ferrari but I also root for Red Bull Racing since I'm also a Honda fan, and they supply engines to Red Bull. I hope one day to attend a Formula One race to see how those cars are driven around the track at very high speeds, and maybe get a closer look at the cars during practice sessions.

My other major interest is mixing music. My uncle used to teach me and my cousins how to use dj equipment and software. Ever since I was taught how to mix, I've never stopped liking it because it's something really fun to do, and being able to perform at my uncle's nightclubs became a very fun activity to do every time I visited them back in Ecuador. I'm currently saving money to buy myself a professional deck for djing, and hope to become a part time dj during the summer.
